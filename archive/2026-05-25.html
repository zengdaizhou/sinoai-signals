The Big Story

Tesla's Full Self-Driving (FSD) technology is poised to make a significant entry into the Chinese market, with XPeng emerging as a potential ally. XPeng's vice president has openly welcomed Tesla's FSD, citing a shared reliance on vision-based autonomous driving systems. This move could validate XPeng's strategic focus on pure vision-based autonomy and intensify competition in the affordable smart-driving segment. However, it also raises questions about how other Chinese automakers will respond to Tesla's advancements, especially as the global race for autonomous driving dominance heats up.
