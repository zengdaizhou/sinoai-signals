The Big Story

DeepSeek, a notable player in the AI industry, has announced a permanent 75% price reduction for its latest flagship model, set to take effect on June 1. This aggressive pricing strategy aims to disrupt the AI hardware market, potentially challenging established giants like Nvidia, SK Hynix, Samsung, and Micron. By significantly lowering costs, DeepSeek seeks to position itself as a formidable competitor, particularly in markets traditionally dominated by these companies. The move underscores a broader trend of Chinese AI firms leveraging cost advantages to penetrate global markets, potentially reshaping the competitive landscape of the AI hardware industry.
